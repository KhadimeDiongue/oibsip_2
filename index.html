Elon Musk

An Innovator, Entrepreneur, and Visionary

Elon Musk is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., and CEO of Twitter. He is known for his ambitious vision to revolutionize transportation both on Earth and in space.

Timeline

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1995: Dropped out of Stanford University after two days to pursue a career in business and technology.
  • 1999: Founded X.com, which later became PayPal.
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX.
  • 2004: Joined Tesla Motors, Inc. as chairman and product architect, becoming its CEO in 2008.
  • 2015: Founded OpenAI.
  • 2016: Founded Neuralink and The Boring Company.
  • 2022: Acquired Twitter.
